About

ShadowCryption is a lightweight tool for learning and demonstrating secure steganography and encryption. Users can hide secret messages inside images and audio files, encrypt them with a password, and share securely.

Technical Details: We use AES encryption with PBKDF2 key derivation and HMAC integrity checks. Messages are embedded into the least significant bits of images (PNG) and audio (WAV) files.

Developed with privacy and usability in mind, ShadowCryption helps users experiment with secure data hiding in an easy-to-use interface.
